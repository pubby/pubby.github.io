An arcade shooter, ostensibly created in 24 hours for the Ludum Dare competition. This game has a huge amount of objects on screen at once - perhaps the most of any NES game.

  • D-Pad moves Earth.
  • A/B rotates Moon.
  • Hit select to use power-ups.
  • Hit start to begin the game.
